What is Valor?

Valor helps you find someone to share adventures with. Search beyond your friend group for other travelers to split costs and make memories. Filter based on travel location, travel dates, age, gender, and lots more!

How it Works

  1. Download Valor
  2. Create your profile and specify preferences:
    • Likes tacos, the beach, festivals, hiking
    • Works 9-5
    • Non smoker
  3. Choose a location to travel to and/or set some travel dates
  4. Find your perfect roomie

Search

Search by location and/or by date.

If you're flexible on dates, search for others who are also interested in the same destination.

If you're only available to travel during a specific window, search for others who are looking during that same time in any location.

The Profile

Explore other traveler's preferences. Make sure they pass the vibe check before connecting and starting your adventure together. Check out previous roommate ratings and any possible mutual connections.

Why Valor?

Flexibility

Search by location, by date, by both, or by neither. Include other filters with each search to guarantee you find the perfect match.

Community

Nothing makes an honest friendship like traveling does. No time for beating around the bush; if you gotta go, you gotta go.

Safety

All users are put through a 3rd party verification system. Additionally, profiles include mutual connections and any ratings from previous roommates.

Save Money

Traveling with a friend and splitting costs can save lots of money!
